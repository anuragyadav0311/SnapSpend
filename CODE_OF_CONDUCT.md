# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making SnapSpend a welcoming, respectful, and productive project for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or perspective.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, reviews, and issues.
- Assume good intent, while taking responsibility for your impact.
- Give and receive feedback with care.
- Keep disagreements focused on the work, not the person.
- Help newcomers understand the project and contribution process.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing credentials, secrets, or sensitive user data.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, commits, discussions, documentation, chat channels, and any public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban contributors who violate this code. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, consistent, and focused on protecting the community.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainers. Include relevant links, screenshots, context, and the names of people involved when possible. Maintainers should handle reports with discretion and avoid unnecessary disclosure.

## Attribution

This code is adapted from common open source community conduct guidelines and the Contributor Covenant spirit of respectful collaboration.
